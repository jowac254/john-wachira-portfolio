Analysis & insights

Titanic Survival Analysis

Exploratory data analysis and survival prediction on the Kaggle Titanic dataset. Examined how passenger class, gender, age, and embarkation point influenced survival rates. Built a logistic regression model achieving 79% accuracy, and visualised findings in Power BI for stakeholder presentation.

Python SQL Power BI
Survival rate
38.4%
Overall survival across 891 passengers in the training dataset
Model accuracy
79%
Logistic regression classifier after feature engineering and encoding
Top predictor
Gender
Female passengers were 74% more likely to survive than male passengers

Key findings

1st class passengers survived at 63% vs 24% in 3rd class — socioeconomic status was a strong survival predictor.
Children under 10 had the highest survival rate across all age groups at 59%.
Passengers who embarked at Cherbourg had a notably higher survival rate, correlated with higher proportion of 1st class tickets.
Power BI dashboard built to allow dynamic filtering by class, gender, and port — enabling non-technical stakeholders to explore the data interactively.
